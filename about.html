Our Story.

Formed in 2002 with the vision of inspiring Africa's Transformation.

The Origin

We are a growing organization with expertise in transformational leadership focused on different key thematic areas.

The Leadership Farm was formed in 2002 and registered in 2008. We believe that leadership can be taught and just like a farmer grows crops based on a science, we can grow leadership that is fit for purpose to deliver strategic goals.

Our passion is to cultivate and unlock the human potential leading to organisational transformation. We empower individuals and organisations to reach new heights.

Our Values

Integrity

We believe that character is the foundation of leadership. Without it, talent is dangerous.

Excellence

We do not accept mediocrity. We strive for world-class standards in everything we do.

Service

True leadership is about serving others, not ruling over them.

Leadership

Oritsejolomi David

Founder & Executive Director

Gbenebichie Oritsejolomi David is a visionary leader, highly regarded speaker, author, mentor, and educator specializing in S.T.A.R. (Servant, Transformational, Authentic, Responsible) leadership. He serves as the Executive Director of The Leadership Farm, driving the vision of inspiring Africa's transformation.

A sought-after consultant, he has collaborated with global organizations including UN Women, UNESCO, Ministry of Education, Airtel, NAPSA, and ZANACO. notably, he served as a lead consultant in establishing the National Transformational Leadership Institute (NTLI) at the University of Juba and the Africa Centre for Transformative and Inclusive Leadership (ACTIL) at Kenyatta University.

Qualifications:
- Honorary Doctorate in Ministry (DMin)
- LL.B BL (Nigeria)
- MBA (Lancaster University, UK)
- Ph.D. in Innovative Urban Leadership (In View, Bakke Graduate University, USA)
