Our Programs

The Seed Academy

For Ages 10-15

The formative years are critical. The Seed Academy is an after-school program designed to instill core values and critical thinking skills early.

  • Public Speaking & Debate Workshops
  • Ethical Leadership Modules
  • Community Service Projects

The Harvest Network

For Ages 16-25

Transitioning to adulthood requires guidance. The Harvest Network connects ambitious young people with mentors and career opportunities.

  • 1-on-1 Mentorship with Industry Leaders
  • University Application Support
  • Internship Placements
